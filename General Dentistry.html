General Dentistry Services

Our Services

  • Routine Check-ups and Cleanings: Regular dental exams and professional teeth cleaning to maintain oral health.
  • Fillings: Treatment for cavities with materials like amalgam, composite, or porcelain.
  • Preventive Care: Fluoride treatments and dental sealants to protect against cavities.
  • X-rays and Diagnostic Imaging: Use of dental X-rays to detect hidden issues like cavities and infections.
  • Extractions: Removal of severely damaged or decayed teeth, including wisdom teeth.
  • Root Canals: Treatment of infected or inflamed tooth pulp to save a damaged tooth.
  • Crowns and Bridges: Restorative treatments to cover damaged teeth or replace missing ones.
  • Dentures: Removable appliances that replace missing teeth.
  • Gum Disease Treatment: Care for gingivitis and periodontitis to improve gum health.
  • Orthodontic Referrals: Referral for braces or aligners if necessary for alignment concerns.
